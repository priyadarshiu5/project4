Welcome to Jaipur – The Pink City

Jaipur, the capital city of Rajasthan, is renowned for its rich cultural heritage, majestic palaces, and vibrant bazaars. It was founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II and is known as the first planned city of India.

The city is home to many historical landmarks such as the Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, and Amber Fort. Jaipur is also famous for its traditional Rajasthani cuisine, colorful festivals, and skilled artisans who create beautiful handicrafts and jewelry.

With a unique blend of old-world charm and modern lifestyle, Jaipur continues to attract tourists from around the globe and stands as a symbol of India’s glorious past.
